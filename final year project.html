Website

This website shows the line, scatter and growth trend of import products from various countries to Malaysia. It displays detailed charts, including line graphs that illustrate the fluctuations in import volumes over time, scatter plots that show the correlation between different product categories and their countries of origin, and growth trend graphs that highlight the overall increase or decrease in imports across different years. These visualizations help users analyze the patterns, identify key trading partners, and understand the dynamics of Malaysia's import activities. This version adds more context about what the website offers and what users can gain from interacting with the data.

Dashboard

The dashboard offers a comprehensive overview of sports product sales across different countries and categories. It provides an interactive platform where users can explore detailed insights into the performance of various sports-related products in the global market. Key features of the dashboard include:
  •   Sales by Country: Bar chart visualizing the total sales figures of sports products in different countries that allows users to compare which countries have the highest or lowest sales.
  •  Sales by Category: A breakdown of sales data by product category. Users can view how different categories perform in terms of sales volume and profit.
  •  Filter Options: Users can filter data by specific timeframes, regions, or product types, making the dashboard customizable to their needs.
